# Voice Direction

## Native Scene Voice

Use this for human/UGC reads:

```text
Perform like a real creator speaking into an iPhone mic in the room. Natural breath, slight imperfection, conversational timing, not a polished ad narrator. The line should sound triggered by what just happened on camera.
```

## Clover Mascot Voice

Use this once Clover is speaking as a recurring character:

```text
Perform as Clover, a tiny overpowered digital employee inside a startup office. Voice is close-mic headset audio: warm, dry, confident, slightly amused, not childish, not robotic, not announcer-like. Speak quickly but clearly, with crisp stops and a tiny smile. The line should feel like it is happening in the room, not narrated over the video.
```

## Audio Mix Notes

- High-pass around `80-120 Hz`.
- Light compression.
- Add a tiny room or headset color.
- Room tone begins before speech.
- Foley lands on visible actions.
- Captions follow speech rhythm if captions are used.
