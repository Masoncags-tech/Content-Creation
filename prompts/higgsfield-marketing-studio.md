# Higgsfield Marketing Studio Prompt Blocks

## UGC Product-Proof Short

```text
Native TikTok/Reels UGC, [8-12] seconds, 9:16.

First frame is already active: [physical prop gag or visual interruption].

Scene: [specific real setting]. Camera: [phone/selfie/handheld/dash mount], imperfect and plausible.

Prop logic: [prop] starts [location], [performer] touches it because [reason], it ends [location]. No other props appear.

Performer says in-scene, not voiceover: "[short line]".

Cut once to proof insert: [phone/calendar/task/checkmark/product proof]. Use icons and visual states only. No readable fake text. If precise UI is needed, keep the screen turned away/black/blurred and add deterministic UI in post.

Cut back to performer for payoff: "[short payoff line]".

Audio: [room tone], [specific foley], close iPhone mic. No polished ad voice, no music unless requested.

Avoid generated captions, end cards, fake logos, readable fake UI text, invented app screens, morphing text, random props appearing, stock ad acting, sci-fi visuals, robots, purple AI gradients.
```

## Clover Car UGC Prompt

```text
Tighter native TikTok/Reels UGC, no generated captions or end-card text. First frame is already active: parked car dash selfie, phone in mount vibrating with generic unreadable notification bubbles, coffee cup wobbling. The coffee lid already sits loose on the cup; the founder grabs it because the buzzing interrupted her. She uses it like a tiny microphone. No other props appear. She says in-scene, with iPhone mic audio and car cabin sound: "I missed the call. Clover didn't." Quick insert: phone screen is angled away with only green check icons visible, no readable words, no app grid, no calendar text. Back to her: tiny relieved laugh, "That is the whole pitch." Realistic creator lighting, dash-mounted iPhone, slight mount wobble, natural mouth sync, no polished ad voice, no subtitles, no readable fake text except the Clover logo if needed. Keep it under 10 seconds, first physical action before second one, one clean cut to the phone insert, then back.
```
