# Scene Design Prompt

## Metadata

- Brand or venture: generic service business
- Format: product-in-life UGC
- Platform: TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Duration: 15 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Model/tool: AI video generator plus deterministic post
- Reference asset: none

## Script Contract

- Viewer: owner/operator who gets customer inquiries while busy
- Pain: responding late makes the business look sloppy
- First visible event: phone buzzes on a workbench while the owner is mid-task
- Spoken trigger: the buzz interrupts the owner and makes them check what happened
- Proof question: did the follow-up actually happen?
- Payoff: the owner sees the task already handled and relaxes

## Scene Contract

- Location: small back office or workbench
- Time of day: late afternoon
- Lighting source: window light and overhead practical light
- Camera source: handheld phone
- Camera position: close over the workbench, slightly imperfect
- Performer: owner/operator
- Wardrobe: work hoodie or plain tee, no brand logos
- Starting frame: hand already reaching toward buzzing phone
- Ending frame: owner nods once, relieved

## Motion Contract

```text
The camera stays handheld and close as the owner reaches for a buzzing phone, stops mid-reach, and notices a green check status card already visible on a separate proof insert. The shot cuts only when the proof insert appears.
```

## Prop Contract

| Prop | Starts Where | Touched By | Action | Reason | Ends Where |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| phone | face down on workbench | owner | buzzes, then stays angled away from camera | creates the interruption without showing fake UI | on workbench |
| pen | beside notebook | owner | owner taps it once while reacting | gives hand motion during the payoff | beside notebook |

## UI/Text Contract

- Any readable text generated by model? `no`
- If text is needed, where will it be rendered in post? deterministic proof insert
- Device screen policy: phone screen remains angled away, black, reflected, blurred, or icon-only
- Proof insert source: post-rendered status card with icons only unless evidence-backed copy is provided

## Audio Contract

- Room tone: quiet office hum
- Voice source: in-scene direct-to-camera
- Dialogue: "I was about to answer that. It was already handled."
- Foley: phone buzz, pen tap, soft chair movement
- Music: none for first test
- Caption strategy: deterministic captions only if needed after voice lock

## Negative Prompt Ledger

```text
no generated captions, no end-card text, no readable fake UI text, no fake logos, no impossible phone apps, no random props appearing, no extra hands, no warped fingers, no dead first frame, no static face, no loose voiceover, no stock ad acting, no abstract flashing boxes
```

## QA Forecast

- Likely failure 1: phone screen may generate fake apps
- Mitigation: keep phone face down or angled away, proof insert added in post
- Likely failure 2: hand may not touch phone cleanly
- Mitigation: ask for simple reach and stop, not complex unlocking/tapping
- Likely failure 3: office may feel staged
- Mitigation: use imperfect handheld camera, practical light, and one real workbench action

## Final Generation Prompt

```text
Native vertical UGC, 15 seconds, handheld phone camera in a small back office at late afternoon. First frame is already active: a phone buzzes face down on a workbench while an owner in a plain work hoodie reaches toward it. The phone screen stays hidden, black, reflected, blurred, or icon-only with no readable app text. A pen sits beside a notebook and is the only other prop. The owner stops mid-reach, looks at camera, and says in-scene with natural office room tone: "I was about to answer that. It was already handled." Cut once to a clean icon-only proof insert added in post, then back to the owner tapping the pen once and nodding, relieved. No generated captions, no end card, no readable fake UI, no fake apps, no logos, no random props, no stock ad acting.
```
