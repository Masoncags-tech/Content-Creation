## Creative rationale

Mason's ask: photoreal humans that feel real interacting with the animated Clover. The repo's
strongest growth lane (real-creator UGC + physical gag + product proof) merged with the mascot
IP: the mascot IS the product demo. Abilities are shown as physical comedy, not UI shots —
no generated readable text (hard rule). Human carries all dialogue (photoreal lip-sync);
mascot stays MUTE (zero cartoon lip-sync risk, Paddington-style charm).
