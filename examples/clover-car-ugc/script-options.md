# Clover Car UGC Script Options

Format: parked car creator UGC with a prop hook and phone proof insert.

## Best Current Line

> I missed the call. Clover didn't.

Why it works:

- six words
- instantly legible pain
- product payoff is implied
- easy to pair with phone/calendar insert
