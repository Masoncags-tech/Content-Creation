# Prop Continuity Ledger

## Purpose

Use this before generation and again during QA.

Every visible prop must have an origin, owner, action, reason, and end state. If the prop cannot pass this ledger, remove it from the scene.

## Rules

- Do not introduce a prop only for a joke unless the scene explains why it is there.
- Do not rely on generated text printed on props.
- Do not let a prop teleport between shots.
- For hand-object interactions, hands should be visible before contact.
- If the prop is proof, it also needs a `templates/proof-insert-spec.md`.

## Fail Questions

- Where did this come from?
- Why would this person touch it now?
- What changed after they touched it?
- Does the prop create a continuity problem after the cut?
- Would the same prop still be in the scene if no camera were present?
